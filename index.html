About

I am currently a graduate student in the Langauge Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon's School of Computer Science (SCS) working with Professor Eric Nyberg.

In my spare time I am an avid runner, I work as an EMT and Firefighter in Pittsburgh and the surrounding areas, I take lots of pictures, and I love SCUBA Diving.

Currently my research is focused on improving the quality of semantic parsing systems, and dialog systems for the purposes of automated question answering.

Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute Ambulance

Positions Held: Captain, Training Committee Chair
Ranks Achieved: Duty Supervisor, Crew Chief (Trainer), Driver (Trainer)
RPI Ambulance was founded in 1977 as the first EMS agency in Troy and the surrounding area. Today, RPIA serves the RPI campus as well as some of the surrounding towns. Mainly responding to on-campus calls, but also responding to mutual aid calls to Brunswick, North Greenbush (including Wynantskill and Defreestville), and Poestenkill. RPI Ambulance is a student run and operated Basic Life Support (BLS) transporting ambulance agency that serves the RPI campus and surrounding communities. We respond to approximately 150 to 200 calls and provide first aid coverage to about 30 special events every academic year.

Edgewood Volunteer Fire Department

Ranks Achieved: Interior Firefighter, Emergency Medical Technician
The Edgewood Volunteer Fire Department was founded as a volunteer fire company to protect the lives, property, and quality of life of the citizens of the Borough of Edgewood. The EVFD responds to fire, medical, and other emergencies in Edgewood and the surrounding Boroughs, as well as the Penn Lincoln Parkway (I-376) stretch that spans from the Squirrel Hill Tunnel to the Forest Hills exit.

Carnegie Mellon University Emergency Medical Services

Ranks Achieved: Responder
CMU EMS is completely run by students and continues to meet state standards of excellence for patient care. With minimum staffing by at least one EMT and all members certified in CPR and First Aid, CMU EMS provides 24-7 medical coverage and has an average response time of under 5 minutes. Members are commited to serving others and receive no compensation for their time. This includes daily shifts, standby for events, and classes for the community.

RPI Science Ambassadors

The RPI Science Ambassadors (SA) are an assembly of undergraduate students focused on inspiring a younger audience to explore the role of science in society. The SA's develop highly engaging presentations and hands-on demonstrations to bring science to life in K-12 classrooms, and help students define the relevance between their classroom learning and real world applications.
